Umlawan Cave

Umlawan Cave, situated in the Lumshnong village, 60 km out of the East of Jowai is an exciting stop for adventure-keen travellers.
The Umlawan cave is joined by two other caves, the Umskor and the Kot-Sati, which gives it its great length of 21 km. The area is also dotted with several other caves that lie within the distance of 60 km, of which only a few have been explored.

History

Umlawan Cave of Meghalaya state is an exciting stop for adventure lovers. It is located in the Lumshnong village, near Jowai. The cave is regarded as the deepest as well as the longest in the entire sub continent with a surveyed length of 21.6 kms. The caves have existed here for centuries but it has become a famous tourist spot and has gained paramount importance relatively recently.
Many of the caves are still unknown and have not yet been explored. Tourists from different places come here to have a view of the spectacular cave.
