Megalithic Bridge on the Um-Nyankanah

The bridge is located in the district of Jaintia hills. It is situated between Jowai and Jarain. The bridge is built over the Um-Nyakanah river. Strong stone pillars very well support the bridge. According to the local people, the bridge was constructed by Jaintia kings who ruled these lands during the medieval period.
